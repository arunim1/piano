The Piano

Originally, we found this piano about to be thrown away in the Cubberley dumpster. A couple of us MakeX mentors hauled it back in on in a rather unstable cart and got to work on fixing it up. I applied for a grant from PA Public Art, and worked with my talented friends to clean up, fix, and repaint the enntirety of the piano piece by piece.
